Welcome to
Heart of Africa Lodge
(Karibuni wote)

The wildebeest migration, is one of the “Seven New Wonders of the World” and also known as The World Cup of Wildlife. If there is a sight you have to experience, this is it!

learn more

Heart of Africa Lodge is an exquisitely beautiful escape offering guests a full-service vacation package.

We are located in Arusha, a short five-minute drive to the only road leading to Tanzania’s incredible national parks in the Northern Safari Circuit. We welcome you to Heart of Africa Lodge whether you are traveling alone, as a couple, with friends, or as a whole family.
